Welcome to the Sharnbrook Parent & Community Collective

A passionate, action oriented group of parents, carers, and supporters working hand-in-hand with school leadership to build something brilliant for our children!

Our Reimagined Mission

At Sharnbrook Academy, we believe in a vibrant, supportive partnership between school and home. We are transitioning away from traditional, smaller scale operations to focus on the "heavy lifting," organising high-impact community initiatives and larger events.

Our goal is to bring the community together and raise significant funding to directly support the school's core projects, extracurricular activities, and memorable milestones that enrich the educational journey of every student.

Partnerships Working directly with school leadership to fund student & teacher extras.

How Can YOU Get Involved?

We appreciate that life with teenagers is busy. There are no expectations of endless meetings. Choose a path that fits you:

1. The Active Core

Have a couple of hours to spare a month?

Join the central committee pool to help outline budgets, brain-storm larger seasonal festivals, and organise our core calendar

2. The Event Volunteer

Want to help on-the-day only?

Join our casual event mailing pool. Help hand out tickets, staff entry tables, or run refreshment stands for just an hour at a specific evening.

3. Skill Supporter

No free hours, but specialised skills?

Help from your home computer. We always need accountants, graphic design support, local business sponsorships, or help setting up Match Funding.
